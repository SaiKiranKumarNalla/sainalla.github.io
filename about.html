The Masterless Warrior — Bound to no lord, loyal only to the craft

Medical technology specialist with over 2 years of translational R&D experience across med-tech startups and hospitals. I translate clinical needs into actionable solutions — from computational models and 3D-printed phantoms to predictive tools for cancer treatment devices.

Trained at École Polytechnique, now completing a PhD at the Université de Toulouse. My work sits at the crossroads of physics, engineering, and oncology — with one foot in the clinic and the other in the code.

Beyond the lab, I'm drawn to the strategy side — healthcare ventures, life sciences consulting, and the question of how deep-tech research becomes something that actually reaches patients.

McKinsey Forward alumnus. Venture capital curious. Always building.
